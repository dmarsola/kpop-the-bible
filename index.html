About Me

Doug Marsola is a devout Christian, husband, and father of 3 wonderful girls. He lives with his family in Ottawa, Canada's capital city.
Trained as a software developer, he experienced a prolonged season of unemployment.
In this process, his pastor recommended that he should be consistent in his devotionals.
And so Doug prayed, fasted, read the Bible, journaled and sought God's will for that season.
K-pop the Bible was born from that experience of brokenness, expectation and listening for what God had for him.
